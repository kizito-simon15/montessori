About Maria Montessori

Maria Montessori Nursery, Pre and Primary School in Iringa, Tanzania, is a distinguished institution committed to nurturing and educating children with excellence. We empower children to transform their innate dreams, skills, and talents into meaningful realities.

Vision: Through quality education, we envision creating a thriving environment where children grow into respectable, high-achieving individuals and responsible citizens.

Mission: To transform lives by providing quality education, generating knowledge, and applying it effectively to serve the community.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for high-quality standards.
  • Initiative: Encouraging proactive learning.
  • Accountability: Promoting responsible stewardship.
  • Inclusivity: Fostering mutual respect and community.
  • Innovation: Nurturing creativity and independence.
