About Victory English Medium

Established on January 5, 2011, in Biharamulo, Kagera, Tanzania, Victory English Medium Pre- & Primary School began with one pre-primary class of 13 students. Today, with 467 students across seven classes, we deliver a holistic, English-medium education aligned with Tanzania‚Äôs national curriculum, fostering academic excellence, moral values, and life skills.

Our conducive environment includes modern facilities like computer labs, a library, safe dormitories, and a dining hall with nutritious meals. We provide reliable school buses for day students, clean water, and annual health check-ups at a local hospital.

Vision: To be a leading institution in Tanzania, shaping future leaders through quality education.

Mission: To provide affordable, high-quality education that nurtures intellectual, social, and emotional growth.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for academic and personal growth.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethics.
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and inclusion.
  • Community: Fostering collaboration and support.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and technology.
